4. Device Permissions & Usage

To provide call recording functionality, VoxNote requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone Access: Required to capture audio during calls and voice notes.

  • Storage Access: Necessary to save, play, and manage recorded audio files on your device.
  • Battery Optimization: We may request to bypass battery optimization to prevent the system from killing the recording service in the background.
